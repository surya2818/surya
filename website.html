Aazadhi ka amrit mahotsav

The Government of India decided to celebrate the 75 years of Independence of India, with great excitement and tribute to freedom fighters. So, they decided to do various programs and the government named the celebration as 'Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav'. Amrit Mahotsav means 75 years of independence.[1] The Government will celebrate it from 2021 to 2022.
